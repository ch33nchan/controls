Cheen & Chittu Delhi Plan

This version is fully day-wise, with numbered stop order, separate interests for Cheen and Chittu, and one interactive route map per day. The maps show each stop as a numbered balloon marker. Every card below also says what the place is for and whether cab, metro, or walking makes the most sense.

Tag Guide

Cheen · toys, collectibles, books, camera-market detours Chittu · food, crafts, decor, shopping, easier pacing Both · iconic Delhi, shared food, common-interest stops

Transport Rule

Default: cab CP day: walk + short cab Use metro only if traffic is bad

Hotel Rule

CP / Janpath / Barakhamba / Patel Chowk Breakfast included Mid-range, clean, easy cab pickup

Day 1 · Friday, 22 May

CP core + Janpath + evening India Gate / Parliament loop

Low-travel start, then central Delhi at golden hour

This fixes the route properly: CP-heavy in the first half, then the government-area monuments in the evening. It keeps the first full day comfortable and gives you the view you wanted around the flag, India Gate and Parliament.

Planned route

`Hotel in CP -> Ram Chander & Sons -> Janpath -> Depaul's -> evening rest -> Kartavya Path / National Flag -> India Gate -> Parliament / Rashtrapati drive -> dinner back in CP`

Timing: `10:00–1:30` CP stops, `1:30–4:45` hotel/rest, `5:15–8:00` evening monument loop.

Day 2 · Saturday, 23 May

South Delhi and central-south mix: toys, books, crafts, art, Sarojini

Cheen and Chittu day together, but without making it feel like two separate trips

This is the day where the toy-store detours are correctly moved out of CP and into the `Aurobindo / Hauz Khas` belt. It also groups `Dilli Haat`, `Art Hub`, `Sarojini`, and the making/art stops into one coherent day.

Planned route

`Hotel in CP -> Jaiman Toys -> Midland Book Shop -> Dilli Haat -> Sarojini Nagar -> Art Hub -> Clay Company -> Nanda Book Service (optional add-on) -> back to CP`

Timing: `10:00–12:00` Hauz Khas belt, `12:30–2:30` Dilli Haat, `3:00–5:00` Sarojini, `5:30 onward` art/creative stops.

Day 3 · Sunday, 24 May

Old Delhi icons, food, market detours, then airport

One controlled Old Delhi run only

This keeps Old Delhi to a single day, starts early, and leaves enough airport buffer. `Sita Ram` and `Gole Hatti` are handled as deliberate food anchors rather than random add-ons.

Planned route

`Hotel checkout -> Sita Ram Diwan Chand -> Red Fort -> Chaudhary Market -> Kabootar Market -> Gole Hatti -> airport`

Timing: `8:30` checkout, `9:00` breakfast, `10:00–12:00` Red Fort, `12:00–1:30` market detours, `1:45` lunch, `2:30` airport departure.
