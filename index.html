Colorful Delhi for Cheen & Chittu

A playful, map-first Delhi plan with day-wise order, numbered stops, separate picks for Cheen and Chittu, and clear travel guidance for cab, metro, or walking.

Language

Tag Guide

Cheen · toys, collectibles, books, camera-market detours Chittu · food, crafts, decor, shopping, easier pacing Both · iconic Delhi, shared food, common-interest stops

Transport Rule

Day 1 · Friday, 22 May

CP core + Janpath + evening India Gate / Parliament loop

Low-travel start, then central Delhi at golden hour

This is the easy first day: CP in the first half, then the iconic government district in the evening.

Planned route

Hotel in CP -> Ram Chander & Sons -> Janpath -> Depaul's -> rest -> Kartavya Path -> India Gate -> Parliament drive -> dinner

Timing: `10:00–1:30` CP stops, `1:30–4:45` rest, `5:15–8:00` monument loop.

Day 2 · Saturday, 23 May

South Delhi mix: toys, books, crafts, art, Sarojini

A shared day that still gives each of you your own thing

The collectible stops move into the Hauz Khas belt, while Dilli Haat, Sarojini and art-led stops keep it fun for Chittu too.

Planned route

Hotel in CP -> Jaiman Toys -> Midland Book Shop -> Dilli Haat -> Sarojini Nagar -> Art Hub -> Clay Company -> Nanda Book Service (optional) -> back to CP

Timing: `10:00–12:00` Hauz Khas belt, `12:30–2:30` Dilli Haat, `3:00–5:00` Sarojini, `5:30 onward` creative stops.

Day 3 · Sunday, 24 May

Old Delhi icons, food, market detours, then airport

One neat Old Delhi run, not an exhausting marathon

Start early, keep the market detours short, and leave enough time for the airport.

Planned route

Hotel checkout -> Sita Ram Diwan Chand -> Red Fort -> Chaudhary Market -> Kabootar Market -> Gole Hatti -> airport

Timing: `8:30` checkout, `9:00` breakfast, `10:00–12:00` Red Fort, `12:00–1:30` markets, `1:45` lunch, `2:30` airport departure.
